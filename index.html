About me

Hi, I'm Menno Ruis, an 18-year-old graphic design student with a strong passion for photography and game design. I've always been drawn to the power of visual storytelling, and I've found my calling in creating compelling visuals that capture the imagination and leave a lasting impact.

My love for photography began at a young age, and I've been honing my skills ever since. I love exploring my surroundings with my camera, finding beauty in unexpected places and capturing moments that might otherwise be missed. Through my lens, I aim to create images that not only look beautiful but also tell a story and evoke an emotional response.

In addition to photography, I'm also deeply passionate about game design. As a gamer myself, I've always been fascinated by the potential for storytelling and world-building within games. I love designing games that immerse players in a rich, engaging world, and I'm constantly exploring new ways to combine my passion for design with my love of gaming.

Overall, I'm excited to see where my passion for photography, game design, and graphic design will take me in the future. I'm eager to continue learning and growing as an artist, and I'm excited to see how my work will impact and inspire others.
